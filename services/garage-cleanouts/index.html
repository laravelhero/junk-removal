Garage Cleanouts — Park Your Car Again

What We Haul

If it's on this list, consider it gone.

  • Cardboard boxes & old storage bins
  • Broken tools & power equipment
  • Bicycles, sports & exercise gear
  • Scrap wood & leftover building materials
  • Old tires & auto parts
  • Lawn mowers & yard equipment
  • Shelving units & workbenches
  • General accumulated clutter

Don't see your item? We probably take it anyway — just ask when you book.

We do the sorting, not you

You don't have to pre-stage a thing. Point us at the pile and our crew separates donate-able items, recyclables and trash on the spot. We load it, you supervise from the driveway with a coffee.

Swept clean when we leave

A ClearOut garage cleanout isn't done until the floor is broom-swept. You're left with open, usable space — ready for the car, a workshop, or whatever you've been wanting that room back for.
